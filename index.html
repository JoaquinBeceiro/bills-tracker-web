iOS Setup

Open the site (pwa.billstracker.app) on Safari, click on the Share button and select “Add to Home Screen” from the popup. Lastly, tap "Add" in the top right corner.

Android Setup

Open the site (pwa.billstracker.app) on Chrome, press the “three dot” icon in the upper right to open the menu. Select “Add to Home screen” and then press the “Add” button in the popup.

Create a spreadsheet

Create a new Google Spreadsheet on the app or copy from here. Copy the spreadsheet ID/URL. Insert your name and the spreadsheet ID/URL on the login form and click on the “Sign in with Google” button.

About

BillsTracker is a PWA built on React that communicates with the Google Spreadsheet APIs to save and read information. The project is frontend only and the credentials are stored in the user's local storage.
